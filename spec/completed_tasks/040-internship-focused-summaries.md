# Internship-Focused Summaries

## Goal

Refocus each posting’s expandable summary on the internship itself rather than its connection to the student.

## Requirements

- Summarize the internship using verified role facts: title, company, location, work arrangement, program or team when available, responsibilities, qualifications, timing, and application deadline.
- Make internship details and role-specific highlights the primary content.
- Keep personal notes as a separate editable area within the expanded posting.
- Do not present connection status, profile fit, or student-specific scoring as the central summary content.
- If a fact is unavailable, omit it or label it unknown; do not infer job responsibilities from the company alone.
- Retain the current concise collapsed presentation and local-only notes persistence.

## Acceptance checks

- Summary text is clearly about the role and its requirements.
- Connection and student-fit information are absent from the primary summary.
- Summaries use only available posting or collector data.
- Tests cover summary generation with complete and sparse posting metadata.
