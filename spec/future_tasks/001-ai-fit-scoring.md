# Future Task: AI Fit Scoring

## Goal

Use AI to score each internship posting against the user's profile, preferences, resume, coursework, and connections.

## Requirements

- Compare postings against skills, coursework, projects, experience, and preferences.
- Explain why a role is a strong, medium, or weak fit.
- Identify missing qualifications or resume gaps.
- Boost roles where the user has a company connection.
- Penalize disliked role types, especially social media management and marketing.

## Output

- A score and explanation for each posting.

## Notes

This should come after the basic collector and deterministic filter are working, so AI is used for judgment rather than basic data plumbing.
